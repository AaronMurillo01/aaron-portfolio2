  • DogPark

    Html, CSS, JavaScript, React, Bootstrap, TypeScript, Express.js, PostgreSQL, Node.js, Restful API

    Introducing DogPark, the "Dogs best friend app" – a social media platform specifically designed for dog enthusiasts. The app allows users to share photos of their dogs, complete with captions, and interact through likes and comments. Additionally, the app features a dog park map for the city of Albuquerque, enabling users to explore local parks. Our team coordinated through GitHub and managed tasks with ClickUp. Our backend supports six entities (profile, post, park, comment, follow, likes) and connects seamlessly to the frontend using React.js and Redux. The website is containerized with Docker for efficient deployment, and the integration of a JSON file ensures an extensive database of Albuquerque parks for users to explore. Experience DogPark, the ultimate app for dog lovers and their furry friends.
